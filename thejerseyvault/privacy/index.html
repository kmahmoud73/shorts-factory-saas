Privacy Policy — The Jersey Vault

Last Updated: 2026-05-11

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how The Jersey Vault ("we", "our", "the App") handles information when you authorize the App to access your YouTube channel via Google OAuth 2.0.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize The Jersey Vault, we request the following Google OAuth scopes on your YouTube channel:

  • youtube.readonly — to read public metadata about videos and playlists on the connected channel
  • youtube.upload — to upload videos to the connected channel
  • youtube.force-ssl — to set metadata, manage playlists, and reply to comments on the connected channel
  • yt-analytics.readonly — to read channel-level analytics on the connected channel

We do NOT access:

  • Other Google services (Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Photos, etc.)
  • Other YouTube channels not explicitly connected to this App
  • Personal contact lists, friends, or social graph data

3. How We Use the Information

The App uses the granted access solely to:

  • Upload videos that the App generates to the connected YouTube channel
  • Set titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails, and playlist assignments for those videos
  • Read back the App's own uploaded videos to detect duplicates and confirm upload success
  • Read analytics for the App's own channel to inform future content decisions
  • Reply to viewer comments on the App's own videos (where enabled)

4. Data Storage

OAuth refresh tokens are stored encrypted on the application server. They are never:

  • Shared with third parties
  • Sold or rented
  • Used outside the scope of the authorized channel

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only while the App is actively serving the connected channel. Tokens are deleted upon channel disconnect or App decommission.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

7. User Rights

You may revoke the App's access at any time from your Google Account at: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will post any updates to this Privacy Policy at this URL with an updated "Last Updated" date.

How It Works
Four stages. Zero humans.
Our pipeline runs every day, automatically. From trend discovery to YouTube upload, every step is handled by specialized AI agents.

🔎

Research

AI agents scan trending topics, analyze YouTube search data, and score opportunities by virality, star power, and event timing.

02
✍

Write

An editorial AI scores and greenights the best stories. A writer agent crafts scripts optimized for retention, with cinematic visual direction.

03

AI-generated images, animated video clips, professional voiceover, animated subtitles, and sound design are assembled into broadcast-quality shorts.

04

AI Content Research

Smart Engagement

Comment reply bot runs 3x daily with on-brand LLM-generated responses. Thread follow-ups continue conversations. X/Twitter cross-posting on every upload. Community posts drive return visits.

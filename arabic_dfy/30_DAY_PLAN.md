# 30-Day Go-To-Market — First 5 Paying Managed Channels
### Shorts Factory · Arabic-first DFY · "Sandy, your AI director on WhatsApp"

**Goal:** 5 paying managed channels (or 3 paid + 2 revenue-share) within 30 days.
**Constraint:** single operator (Khal) + Sandy + the factory. So the plan leans on *automation, proof, and warm/MENA networks* — not a sales team.
**North-star metric:** booked strategy calls → sample clip sent → close.

---

## The asset you already have = your sales pitch
You're not pitching a promise — you're pitching **live proof**: 8 channels running, the Goha/IYB Arabic ~6× data, the published tales, and Sandy demoing live on WhatsApp. Every prospect should *meet Sandy* and *see a real channel* before they decide. That's the whole funnel.

---

## WEEK 1 — Proof, package, pipeline
- [ ] **Publish the landing page** (`landing.html`) at `shortsfactory.io/managed` (or a sub-page). Add the WhatsApp "meet Sandy" link + Calendly for strategy calls.
- [ ] **Stand up Stripe SKUs:** Launch $497, keep Growth $997 / Scale $1,997, add the Revenue-Share $297+30% product + payment link.
- [ ] **Build the 90-second demo video** *with the factory itself* — screen-record a clip getting approved by voice note in WhatsApp, then going live. This is the single highest-leverage asset.
- [ ] **One-page case study:** the Arabic 6× result + 2 published Goha tales (real links). PDF + a Short.
- [ ] **Niche shortlist (pick 5 to pitch):** Arabic kids' folklore, Gulf true-crime, Arabic "body science"/health-curiosity, Islamic history, MENA football nostalgia, Arabic finance/hustle. Hungry audiences, low competition.
- [ ] **Warm list:** write down every person/business in your network who wants reach in MENA (creators, clinics, e-com, coaches, agencies — your elev8/ojz/qatar contacts are leads).

## WEEK 2 — Outbound + the "free sample" engine
- [ ] **The irresistible opener:** offer a **free custom sample clip** in the prospect's niche/brand, made in 24h. (Cost to you ≈ a few $; conversion lever is huge.) "Tell me your niche, I'll send you a finished Arabic Short tomorrow — no charge."
- [ ] **DM 30 warm + semi-warm leads** with the demo video + free-sample offer. Track in the Lead Command Center.
- [ ] **3 "founder" Shorts** on your own account about the journey ("I built an AI that runs YouTube channels in Arabic") — content marketing that the factory produces. Soft CTA to the landing page.
- [ ] **Agency angle:** approach 3 MENA social/marketing agencies with the **white-label** pitch (one sale = many channels). Higher-value, faster scale.
- [ ] Target: **8–10 booked strategy calls.**

## WEEK 3 — Close + onboard
- [ ] **Run the calls:** structure = (1) their goal, (2) show the live channel + 6× data, (3) demo Sandy live, (4) hand them their free sample, (5) pick a plan. Lead with **Revenue-Share for the hesitant** (kills risk), monthly for the eager.
- [ ] **Onboarding flow (make it 1 day):** niche + language locked → channel created → Sandy added to their WhatsApp → first 3 clips queued → they approve → live. Sub-1-week promise kept.
- [ ] **Close target: 3 paying.** Each new channel = a `routing.json` entry; Sandy routes their messages to their channel — the Ahmad model, replicated.
- [ ] Quota/infra check: each new channel needs its own GCP project/token (mind the YouTube quota lesson). Stage these as you sign.

## WEEK 4 — Prove, systematize, ask for more
- [ ] **First results to clients** (views/subs) → screenshots → testimonials. Even early numbers close the next batch.
- [ ] **Referral ask + incentive:** "Know anyone who wants a channel? One month free for both." MENA networks are tight — referrals will be your #1 channel.
- [ ] **Close 2 more** (→ 5 total) from week-2 follow-ups + referrals.
- [ ] **Productize onboarding into a checklist** so #6–#20 take you near-zero time. Document what Sandy does per client so a low-cost curator (an "Ahmad") could run several.
- [ ] **Decide the scale lever:** double down on **white-label/agencies** (fastest multiplier) and/or hire 1–2 curators to manage clusters of channels.

---

## The numbers (rough)
- 30 outbound + 3 founder Shorts + landing → **~8–10 calls** → **~5 closes** (50% with free sample + live proof is realistic for warm/MENA).
- 5 channels: blend of $497–$1,997 ⇒ **~$3.5–6k MRR** by day 30, at high margin (factory cost is a few $ per video).
- Real prize: the **repeatable unit** (curator + AI factory + Sandy routing) + **white-label** — that's how 5 becomes 50.

## Biggest risks (and the fix)
- **Support/scale as a solo op** → fix = the Ahmad model (cheap curators) + white-label (let agencies absorb support).
- **YouTube quota across many channels** → fix = per-channel GCP projects, staged; request bumps early.
- **Monetization takes time** → fix = Revenue-Share framing sets the right expectation; sell the *audience-building* outcome, not instant cash.

---

### The one thing that matters most
**Get prospects to meet Sandy and see a live channel.** Everything else is detail. The product demos itself — your job in 30 days is just to put it in front of the right MENA eyeballs and let the free sample + the 6× proof do the closing.

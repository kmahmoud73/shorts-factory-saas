# Outreach + Free-Sample Templates
### Week-2 engine: DM → free sample → call → close. Bilingual (EN/AR). Keep it human, short, give value first.

> **Rule:** lead with the **free sample clip**, never with the pitch. The sample does the selling. Track every send in the Lead Command Center.

---

## 1) Cold/warm DM — short version (EN)
> Hey [Name] — I built an AI that runs faceless YouTube channels in Arabic (it gets ~6× the views vs English). I'm offering a few people a **free sample clip** in their niche, made in 24h, no strings.
> What niche would you want a channel in? I'll send you a finished Short tomorrow. 🎬

## 1-AR) النسخة العربية
> أهلين [الاسم] 👋 صنعت ذكاء اصطناعي بيشغّل قنوات يوتيوب بدون وجه بالعربي — وبيجيب مشاهدات أضعاف الإنجليزي.
> بعمل عيّنة **فيديو مجاني** في مجالك خلال ٢٤ ساعة، بدون أي التزام. شو المجال اللي بتحب قناة فيه؟ وبكرة بوصلك فيديو جاهز 🎬

---

## 2) After they reply with a niche (deliver the value)
> Love it. Sending you a finished Arabic Short on **[niche]** within 24h. Quick Q so I nail it: who's the audience (country/age), and any brand or angle you want?
> *(Then: build the sample with the factory, send it via WhatsApp.)*

**Delivery message (with the clip):**
> Here's your free sample — fully done by Sandy (my AI director): trends → script → Arabic voiceover → animation → captions. 🎬
> If you like it, I can have this publishing **daily** on a channel for you, and you'd just approve clips by voice note. Want me to show you how it'd run for [niche]? 15 min call: [Calendly]

---

## 3) Founder-content hook (post on your own account, soft CTA)
> I let an AI run a YouTube channel in Arabic for [X] days. It writes, voices, animates, and publishes on its own — I just approve clips by WhatsApp voice note. Here's what happened 👇
> *(Show the real numbers + the voice-note demo. CTA: "Want one for your niche? DM me — free sample.")*

---

## 4) Agency / white-label opener (higher value)
> Hi [Name] — you run content for MENA clients. I built an engine that produces + publishes faceless Arabic Shorts daily, fully managed, approved over WhatsApp.
> I'm letting a few agencies **white-label** it — your brand, your clients, wholesale per channel. One free sample channel to test, on me. Worth a 20-min look?

---

## 5) Follow-up (no reply, day 3) — keep it light
> No worries if the timing's off — the free-sample offer stands whenever. Just send me a niche and I'll make you one. 🙏

## 6) Follow-up after sample, no close (day 4)
> Did the sample land okay? Happy to tweak the voice/style — that's the whole point, you just tell Sandy what you want. If it's useful, the next step is just letting it run daily. Want to try a week?

---

## Strategy call — 5-beat structure (keep to ~20 min)
1. **Their goal** — "what would a channel do for you — reach, leads, brand, revenue?"
2. **Proof** — show a live channel + the ~6× Arabic stat (30s).
3. **Meet Sandy live** — send a clip in the call, approve it by voice note on screen. *(This is the close.)*
4. **Hand them the sample** — their free clip, already made.
5. **Pick a lane** — Revenue-Share for the hesitant ("you pay almost nothing till it earns"), monthly for the eager. Send the Stripe/payment link before you hang up.

---

## Targeting (where to send the DMs)
- **Warm network first** — anyone in your MENA contacts wanting reach (clinics, e-com, coaches, creators; your elev8 / ojz / qatar / barca contacts).
- **Niche creators** stuck doing English / burning out on editing.
- **MENA marketing agencies** (white-label).
- **SMBs** that "should be on YouTube but have no time" — the DFY promise is built for them.

> Volume target Week 2: **30 personalized DMs + 3 founder posts → 8–10 booked calls.** The free sample is the unlock — almost nobody says no to a free finished video in their niche.

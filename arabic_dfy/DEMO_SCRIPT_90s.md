# 90-Second Demo Video — "Meet Sandy, your AI content director"
### The single highest-leverage sales asset. Screen-record it on the phone (WhatsApp) + a YouTube tab.

**Goal:** in 90 seconds, a stranger *gets it* — "I text an AI, it makes and publishes my channel." Show, don't tell. Real screens, no slides.

**Format:** vertical (9:16) for Shorts/Reels/Status, AND a 16:9 cut for the landing page. Screen-recording of a real phone + a quick YouTube tab. Your voiceover (calm, confident, ~140 wpm).

---

## Shot list + voiceover (timed)

**[0:00–0:08] — Hook (face or text on screen)**
> *VO:* "This is a YouTube channel that runs itself. I never edit a thing — I just send a voice note. Watch."
*Screen:* the channel page with a few published Shorts (real views visible).

**[0:08–0:25] — A clip arrives for review (WhatsApp)**
*Screen:* WhatsApp chat with "Sandy". A new video message lands with an Arabic caption ("جاهز للمراجعة 🎬").
> *VO:* "Sandy — my AI director — just finished a clip. Trends, script, voiceover, animation, captions — all automatic. It lands in WhatsApp for my okay."
*Action:* tap play — 2–3 seconds of the actual Short playing (Arabic, captions on).

**[0:25–0:45] — Approve / tweak by voice note**
*Screen:* hold the mic, record a short voice note: *"حلوة، بس خلّي الصوت أوضح، وبعدها انشرها"* (nice, make the audio clearer, then publish).
> *VO:* "I reply by voice — in any dialect. It understands, fixes it, and publishes. No dashboard. No editing."
*Screen:* Sandy replies "تمام 👍 ظبطت الصوت ونشرتها ✅" with a youtu.be link.

**[0:45–1:05] — It's live + cross-posted**
*Screen:* tap the link → the video is LIVE on YouTube. Quick cut to it also on TikTok/Reels.
> *VO:* "Live on YouTube in seconds — and auto-posted to TikTok, Reels and Snapchat. Same clip, five platforms."

**[1:05–1:25] — The Arabic edge + proof**
*Screen:* a simple stat card: "Arabic ≈ 6× the views of English."
> *VO:* "And here's the secret: in Arabic, the same idea pulls about six times the views. The market's wide open — and Sandy is native Arabic."

**[1:25–1:30] — CTA**
*Screen:* text — "Want a free sample clip for YOUR niche? WhatsApp Sandy."
> *VO:* "Tell me your niche — I'll send you a finished Arabic Short tomorrow, free."

---

## Notes
- **Use a REAL published tale** (e.g. القطة واللحمة / the donkey tale) so views are genuine.
- Keep the voice-note moment center-stage — *that's the wow*. Linger on the mic + the instant reply.
- Subtitle the whole thing (people watch muted). Burn EN subs on the AR cut and vice-versa.
- End frame = the free-sample offer + the WhatsApp/Calendly link. Always be giving the sample.
- 3 cuts to produce: **90s landing (16:9)**, **45s Short (9:16)**, **15s teaser** for Status/Stories.

Performance-Based Refund

If we fail to deliver the agreed deliverables within your first 30 days, you are entitled to a full 100% refund regardless of timing.

Deliverables include: pipeline setup, channel configuration, content production at the agreed cadence, and automated uploads. If the pipeline is non-functional or consistently underperforming due to our fault, you get a full refund.

What Qualifies for a Refund

  • Pipeline fails to produce videos at the agreed cadence for 7+ consecutive days due to our technical issues.
  • Videos are consistently low quality (broken audio, corrupt files, wrong content) and we fail to fix the issue within 48 hours of being notified.
  • Service is materially different from what was described at purchase.
  • We are unable to set up the pipeline for your channel within 14 days of onboarding.

What Does NOT Qualify

  • YouTube algorithm performance (views, subscribers, engagement) — we control content quality, not YouTube's distribution.
  • YouTube policy actions (strikes, demonetization) resulting from your pre-existing channel content or actions.
  • Changes to third-party APIs (YouTube, AI services) that temporarily affect production.
  • Dissatisfaction with a single video (we'll remake it, not refund for it).
  • Failure to provide required access (YouTube OAuth, channel credentials) in a timely manner.
